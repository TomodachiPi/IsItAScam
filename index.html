Deepfake

Deepfakes are videos, images, or audio made using artificial intelligence to make someone look or sound like they did something they never did. They can be used for scams, fake news, and spreading false information. Because deepfakes look so real, it’s getting harder to tell what’s true and what’s fake online.

Call/SMS Scam

SMS and call scams are fraudulent attempts to trick individuals into providing personal information, financial details, or access to accounts. Scammers often impersonate banks or government agencies. Common tactics include fake prize notifications, account verification requests, or claims of suspicious activity.
